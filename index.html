VLOG #3

One of the best experience in Negros

One of the most beautiful falls I have ever seen. It was just one and half hours drive from Bacolod city. At first I was skeptical if water was really turquoise as seen on Youtube but my fam and I were amazed as it really was! Water is a bit cold and rocks are slippery so just take extra careful. Also, be prepared with the 108 steps ascending from the falls going back to the cottages. I find that two trips back and forth was a great workout for the day. You can take a few steps at a time though as long as no one is behind you as you would definitely cause a delay if you stop.
